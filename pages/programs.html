Programs

Developmental Program

Short term plan (First 6 months)
Teach the young boys and girls the game of football from the foundation. Teaching them the basic skill in the game of football which include : passing, controlling the ball, heading, dribbling etc.


Give the players proper drills, which would make them physically and mentally fit. Prepare the pupils for friendly (exhibitions) match with other schools in Thrissur.


Long term plan (second 6 months and beyond)
1. Intense training.
2. More practice (exhibitions) matches.
3. Enrol the team in district, state and national league team.


What the football academy needs for effective and result oriented training session
• A good playing field
• About 1 ball for each player
• Minimum 2 sets of bibs
• Different types of cones, poles, shades, hurdles etc.


Junior Football Development (Ages 13-17)
Designed for serious young footballers, this program emphasizes advanced techniques, tactical understanding, and physical conditioning.

Additional Activities
We offer beach training, trekking, motivation classes, nutrition classes, and physiotherapy sessions to ensure holistic development.

Frequent Matches and Tournaments
Regularly scheduled matches and tournaments help our players gain valuable experience and exposure.

Our Promise

• Our main priority is the happiness of children
• We would teach the children the game of football from the rudiment.
• Our instructors have experience in passing instruction/teaching to pupils.
• We are partners for life not for now.
